High Security Registration Plate (HSRP) Mandatory Rules: HSRP is a mandatory requirement for all registered vehicles in Uttarakhand. It consists of a chromium-based hologram, a unique laser-branded serial number, and non-reusable snap locks. Driving a vehicle without HSRP can attract a penalty of up to Rs. 5,000.

How to Book HSRP Online and Offline: Online: Visit the official authorized portal (bookmyhsrp.com). Enter your state (Uttarakhand), vehicle registration number, engine number, and chassis number. Select the vehicle class (two-wheeler/four-wheeler) and fuel type. Choose either center fitment at an authorized local automobile dealer in Dehradun or home delivery fitment (an additional charge of ~Rs. 125 for bikes, ~Rs. 250 for cars). Pay the fee online (approx Rs. 531 for two-wheelers, Rs. 879 for cars). Book a date and time slot. Offline: Visit any authorized vehicle dealer of your vehicle brand in Dehradun with your RC and request manual booking. The dealer will log it online and allocate the appointment.

Fancy/VIP Registration Number Booking and e-Auction: Custom or VIP registration numbers (e.g. 0001, 0007, 7777) are allotted through an online e-auction process on the Parivahan Fancy Number portal (vahan.parivahan.gov.in/fancy). First, register on the portal and check availability. Pay the registration/booking fee online to get a Unique Acknowledgement Number (UAN). The bidding process runs online. If there are multiple bidders, the highest bidder at the end of the auction is allocated the number. The bidder must pay the remaining balance online within 3 days. Super Elite numbers (0001) start at Rs. 5 Lakhs, single-digit numbers (0002-0009) start at Rs. 1 Lakh, and other popular numbers range from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 75,000.

Pollution Under Control (PUC) Certificate Rules: Every motor vehicle operating on Dehradun roads must carry a valid Pollution Under Control (PUC) certificate. New vehicles have a PUC validity of 1 year from the date of purchase. For older vehicles, PUC must be renewed every 6 months. To obtain a PUC, take the vehicle to any authorized PUC testing center (usually found at major petrol pumps in Dehradun). The technician will insert a probe into the exhaust pipe to measure carbon monoxide and hydrocarbon emissions. If compliant, a digital PUC certificate is printed, and details are uploaded to the Vahan database. The fee is Rs. 50 for two-wheelers, Rs. 80 for petrol cars, and Rs. 100 for diesel cars. Driving without a valid PUC attracts a fine of Rs. 10,000 for the first offense.

Commercial Vehicle Fitness Certificate (FC) Rules: All transport and commercial vehicles (autos, taxis, commercial pickup trucks, buses) must hold a valid Fitness Certificate (Form 38) to operate. For new commercial vehicles, the FC is valid for 2 years. For vehicles older than 8 years, the fitness certificate must be renewed every year.

Commercial Vehicle Fitness Certificate (FC) Application Process: Online: Go to the Vahan portal, select "Vehicle Related Services", select "Apply for Fitness Certificate", upload the current RC, insurance, PUC, and permit details. Pay the inspection fee online. Book a slot for the physical inspection. Offline: Visit RTO Dehradun, purchase Form 38, fill it out, and submit it along with copies of RC, insurance, tax receipt, permit, and PUC. Pay the fee in cash at the counter. Report to the RTO inspection bay with the commercial vehicle. The motor vehicle inspector will physically inspect the brakes, lights, tires, speed governor (speed limiting device fitment), steering, and structural integrity. If passed, the inspector approves the fitness in the Vahan system, and the certificate is printed.

Commercial Vehicle Permits in Uttarakhand: To operate a commercial vehicle (like local taxis, autos, commercial cargo trucks, tourist buses), you must obtain a permit. The different types of permits are:
1. Local Auto/Taxi Permit (valid for Dehradun city or specific local zones).
2. State Carriage Permit (valid for operating across Uttarakhand state).
3. National Permit (valid for transport cargo trucks operating across multiple Indian states).
Permit applications must be made on the Vahan portal or physically submitted to Room 22 (Permits section) at RTO Dehradun using specific permit application forms, along with vehicle RC, insurance, fitness certificate, road tax receipt, local address proof, and the prescribed fee. The permit is typically issued for 5 years.

High Security Registration Plate (HSRP) Helpdesk: For any HSRP plate fitment errors, delay queries, or home delivery status updates in Dehradun, citizens can visit the HSRP Helpdesk. This is located as a dedicated window inside the main entrance lobby corridor of the RTO Dehradun office building.

RTO Dehradun Senior Officers and Administration Contacts: For appeals, administrative disputes, special approvals, or complaints, citizens can visit senior officers at RTO Dehradun. The Zonal Head and Regional Transport Officer (RTO Dehradun) is located in Room 1. The Assistant Regional Transport Officer (ARTO Admin & Registration Authority) is located in Room 2. The Motor Vehicle Inspectors (MVI) who conduct vehicle physical inspections and sign fitness approvals are stationed in the Inspection Bay / Testing Track at the rear of the main RTO building.
