RTO Dehradun Master Office Rooms and Counters Directory Layout: The Regional Transport Office (RTO) Dehradun is located at Rajpur Road. Navigating the physical building is organized by designated room numbers and counters.

RTO Dehradun Room 1: This is the office of the Senior Regional Transport Officer (RTO). It handles zonal administration, senior appeals, policy decisions, and visitor grievances or complaints.

RTO Dehradun Room 2: This is the office of the Assistant Regional Transport Officer (ARTO). It handles registration authorizations, licensing disputes, and official administrative approvals.

RTO Dehradun Room 4: This is the Vahan Registration Branch. All vehicle registration services, including ownership transfers (RC Transfer), RC Renewals, Duplicate RC issuance, Hypothecation addition or termination (Form 34/35 loan cancellation), and other-state vehicle re-registration (UK-07) are processed at the counters in Room 4.

RTO Dehradun Room 14: This is the Enforcement and Challan Section. It handles checking pending traffic police or RTO e-challans, clearing impounded vehicles, and manual/offline fine settlements.

RTO Dehradun Room 22: This is the Commercial Vehicle Permits Section. It processes permits for local transport autos, Vikram tempos, commercial passenger buses, and national cargo truck permits.

RTO Dehradun Room 25: This is the Sarathi Licensing Branch's central biometrics room. It is the hub for Permanent Driving License (DL) submissions and biometric capture (photo and signature registration). It also houses the counters for permanent DL inquiries.

RTO Dehradun Room 26: This is the licensing document verification room. Counters inside verify physical application files and documents for both Learner's Licenses (LL) and Permanent DLs.

RTO Dehradun Room 27: This is the licensing approval desk. Licensing authorities inside review and approve Learner's License and permanent DL applications in the Sarathi database.

RTO Dehradun Room 28: This is the DL Printing, Dispatch, and Backlog room. It handles printing smart card driving licenses, preparing Speed Post dispatches, and processing backlog entries (entering old manual booklet licenses into the digital Sarathi database).

RTO Dehradun Room 29: This is the Learner's License (LL) Computer Screen Test Room. It houses the exam terminal systems where applicants sit for their online LL road rules test.

RTO Dehradun Counter 3: This is the New Vehicle Self-Registration Counter, located on the ground floor registration windows, where custom self-registration files are submitted.

RTO Dehradun Cash Section: This is the fee payment lobby, located in the central ground-floor reception area. All manual taxes, licensing fees, and registration payments are paid at these cash counters in exchange for physical receipts.

RTO Dehradun Inspection Bay and Test Track: Located in the rear compound area of the building. The Motor Vehicle Inspectors (MVI) conduct physical fitness checks for commercial vehicles and supervise the physical driving skill tests for permanent DL applicants.
